THE ROLE
We're looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our Core ML team. You'll be 
responsible for designing, building, and deploying production ML systems that serve millions 
of predictions daily. This is a high-impact role where you'll work on challenging problems 
at the intersection of ML research and engineering.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Design and implement production-grade ML models for classification, NLP, and recommendation systems
• Lead ML projects from research and experimentation through production deployment
• Build and maintain ML infrastructure including training pipelines, feature stores, and model serving
• Collaborate with product managers and engineers to define ML-powered features
• Mentor junior team members and contribute to engineering best practices
• Stay current with ML research and evaluate new techniques for business applications
• Participate in on-call rotation for ML systems
• Write technical documentation and present to stakeholders

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
• 5+ years of experience in machine learning engineering or applied ML research
• Strong proficiency in Python and ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, JAX)
• Experience building and deploying ML models in production environments
• Expertise in at least one ML domain: NLP, Computer Vision, or Recommendation Systems
• Solid understanding of ML fundamentals: optimization, regularization, model evaluation
• Experience with MLOps tools and practices (MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar)
• Strong software engineering skills including testing, code review, and documentation
• Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related quantitative field

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
