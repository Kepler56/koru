JOB TITLE: Data Analyst
COMPANY: RetailGiant Corp.
LOCATION: New York, NY (Onsite)
JOB TYPE: Full-time
SALARY: $80,000 - $110,000 + Annual Bonus

ABOUT RETAILGIANT
RetailGiant Corp. is one of America's largest retail chains with over 2,000 stores 
nationwide and a rapidly growing e-commerce presence. We serve millions of customers 
daily and are committed to delivering exceptional value and customer experience. 
Our analytics team plays a crucial role in driving data-informed decisions across 
merchandising, marketing, and operations.

ROLE OVERVIEW
We're seeking a Data Analyst to join our Business Intelligence team. In this role, 
you'll analyze large datasets to uncover insights that drive business strategy, 
create dashboards for stakeholders, and support cross-functional teams with 
data-driven recommendations. This is an excellent opportunity for someone who 
loves turning data into stories that impact business decisions.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Analyze large datasets (10M+ rows) to identify trends, patterns, and actionable insights
• Create and maintain interactive dashboards and reports for business stakeholders
• Support marketing team with campaign performance analysis and customer segmentation
• Partner with merchandising team on pricing analysis and inventory optimization
• Develop and automate recurring reports, reducing manual effort
• Conduct ad-hoc analyses to answer business questions
• Present findings to both technical and non-technical audiences
• Collaborate with data engineering team on data quality initiatives
• Document analysis methodologies and maintain data dictionaries

QUALIFICATIONS

Required:
• Bachelor's degree in Business, Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, or related field
• 2+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence role
• Expert proficiency in Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, complex formulas)
• Strong SQL skills for querying relational databases
• Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau or Power BI)
• Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities
• Strong written and verbal communication skills
• Attention to detail and ability to manage multiple projects

Preferred:
• Python or R programming experience for data analysis
• Experience in retail, e-commerce, or consumer goods industry
• Knowledge of statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
• Familiarity with Google Analytics or Adobe Analytics
• Experience with A/B testing and experimentation
• MBA or advanced degree in quantitative field
• Experience with cloud data warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift)

TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES
• Analysis: Excel, SQL, Python, R
• Visualization: Tableau, Power BI, Looker
• Data: Snowflake, SQL Server, Google Analytics
• Collaboration: Jira, Confluence, Slack
